## What is TR?

# TR stands for Ton of Refrigeration and is a standard unit used to measure cooling capacity.

## How often should PVC fills be replaced?

# PVC fills typically need replacement every 3 to 7 years, depending on water quality and operating conditions.

## What causes high outlet water temperature?

# Common causes include clogged fills, damaged nozzles, poor fan performance, scaling, and improper water flow.

## What causes cooling tower vibration?

# Vibration can result from fan imbalance, bearing wear, motor issues, or structural looseness.
